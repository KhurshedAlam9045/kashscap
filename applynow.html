Your Loan Journey Begins Here: What You’ll Need

Age Eligibility

To apply, you must be 18 or older and have a history of credit usage.

Basic Income Requirement

You must have an annual income of £10,000 or more from employment or a pension.

Have a Supported Bank

Your bank must be UK-registered. Click here to view the list of accepted banks.

Have an iOS or Android Device

If you accept a loan offer, you will need a compatible mobile device to download our app and manage your loan. Your device must be running iOS 16 or newer, or Android 7.0 or newer.
